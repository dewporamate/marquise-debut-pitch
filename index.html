02 — Market Proof & Inspiration

What Fans Expect

Insights from KATSEYE & YUPP! Trainee Debut Live references

REFERENCE 01

KATSEYE — 1st Anniversary Live VOD

HYBE × GEFFEN

KATSEYE's 1st Anniversary Live on YouTube — a Sit-Down Interview mixed with Interactive Games that set the gold standard for authentic fan connection.

KEY LEARNINGS
  • Real-Time Comment Interaction
    Fans felt part of the show through real-time comment reading broadcast live on air.
  • Authentic Unscripted Moments
    Unscripted segments (Trivia, Games, Letter Reading) consistently drove the highest engagement.
  • Multi-Camera Coverage
    Fluid camera cuts made viewers feel like they were inside the studio — not just watching a screen.
  • Milestone Storytelling
    Fans want to witness milestone moments — the bigger the first, the higher the standard must be.
REFERENCE 02

What's YUPP! EP.29 — Trainee Debut Live

YUPP! Original

The first-ever YUPP! Trainee debut live — a Sit-Down Reveal at YUPP! Studio that serves as the direct blueprint for MARQUISE.

KEY LEARNINGS
  • Debut Live = The First Memory
    200+ viewers joined from minute one — everyone knew this was an unrepeatable moment.
  • Studio Size ≠ Production Quality
    YUPP!'s compact studio produced a premium Debut Live through smart camera blocking and precise lighting.
  • Comment = Live Energy
    The host read audience comments throughout — keeping the studio atmosphere electric and alive.
  • Thai & International Fans — Ready to Cheer
    YUPP!'s fanbase is watching from the edge of their seats from live one — this moment cannot be missed.
CORE INSIGHT FROM BOTH REFERENCES

Fans don't just watch "the artist" — they come to be part of a historic moment.
